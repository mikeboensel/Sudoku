# Sudoku
Simple Solver

Takes input of the format:
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 ----------------------
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 ----------------------
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _  
 _ _ _  | _ _ _  | _ _ _ 

Where '_' are unknown cell values
'|' and '-' are purely cosmetic and not required.
